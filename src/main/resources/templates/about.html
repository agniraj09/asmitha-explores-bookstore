About Asmitha Explores 🌟

A mom, a teacher, and a growing community of little learners

Our Story

Hi! I'm Pavithra — a school teacher by profession and a mom by heart. 💛 Asmitha Explores started as something very simple: I wanted to share the ways I was teaching my daughter Asmitha at home, making things fun, visual, and easy to grasp.

I started posting on Instagram — just small videos of how I explained alphabets, phonics, Tamil letters, and everyday concepts to Asmitha. I didn't expect much. But parents started reaching out, saying their kids loved it too. The community grew, and so did the love for learning together!

What you see in this store — flash cards, activity books, writing boards, story books — these are the same materials I used with Asmitha. Every product is something I personally believe in, because I've seen it work with my own child. 🎉
